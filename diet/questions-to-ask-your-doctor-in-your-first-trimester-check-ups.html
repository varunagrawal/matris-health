questions to ask your doctor in your first trimester check-ups

The first three months of your pregnancy are known as your 'first trimester'. It is probably the time of the most anxiety and excitement, especially if this is your first baby. But even if you have been pregnant before, you will have many questions because each pregnancy is different.

The questions could be about changes (physical, mental and/or emotional) that your body will go through or about simple things like "eating habits" or "exercise". No question is stupid and it is important you have a doctor you feel comfortable enough to talk to. If you are not happy with your doctor, or are looking for one, see our guide to choosing your gynaecologist.

Asking questions during your checkups is important to help you understand what to expect and to understand what is happening with you and your baby. Here is a list of possible questions for the first trimester to act as a memory jogger. You won't want to ask all of them. It's unlikely you will have time as most doctors are busy. Read through the ones that interest you and then decide if there are more questions you want to ask your doctor.

  • What should I change in my eating habits now that I am pregnant?
  • Is there a diet that will help me handle my morning sickness?
  • Can I have street food, artificial sweeteners and aerated drinks during this time?
  • My mother says that spotting is normal, but I'm scared. What should I do?
  • Can I follow a completely vegetarian diet?
  • What antenatal vitamins should I take?
  • Can I climb the stairs and clean the house during my pregnancy?
  • Can I continue working?
  • Is it safe to go on working night shifts?
  • What exercise is safe?
  • Are there any medicines I should avoid?
  • How much weight should I gain during the first three months?
  • What tests do I need to take during this time?
  • What kind of clothes should I invest in? Is it safe to wear a maternity support belt?
  • What situation(s) should I consider as an emergency and call my doctor?
  • Is it safe to have sex in the first trimester?
  • Can I travel by car, bus, two wheeler, train, sea or air during this period?
  • Can I fast? Are there any precautions I should take during festivals? Can I play Holi?
  • Is it alright to dye my hair or use bleach cream? Is going to the beauty parlour safe?
  • Can I drink and smoke in moderation?
